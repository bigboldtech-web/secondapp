The iPhone 11 still holds up remarkably well in 2026. The A13 Bionic chip handles everything from social media to light gaming without breaking a sweat. The dual camera system takes excellent photos, and iOS updates keep it secure.

**What to check:** Look for screen burn-in on AMOLED displays. Open a plain white image and check for ghost images.

The Nord 2 is a solid all-rounder with the MediaTek Dimensity 1200 chip, 65W fast charging, and a clean OxygenOS experience. Charging from 0 to 100% takes about 30 minutes.

**What to check:** Verify the IMEI number against OnePlus's official database.

## Tips for buying used phones

- **Always check the IMEI** — make sure the phone isn't reported stolen
- **Ask for the original bill** — proves ownership and helps with warranty claims
- **Test all buttons and ports** — charge port, volume, power, speakers
- **Check for water damage** — look at the SIM tray for a red indicator
- **Buy from verified sellers** — Second App's certification system ensures dealer accountability
